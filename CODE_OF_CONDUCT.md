# Code of Conduct

**Last Updated:** 08/03/2026 | **Maintained By:** Syntek Development Team

---

## Our Commitment

Syntek Studio is committed to maintaining a welcoming, inclusive, and professional environment for
everyone who contributes to or participates in this project, regardless of background or experience
level.

---

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in all communication
- Focus on ideas and code, not individuals
- Accept feedback gracefully and offer it kindly
- Acknowledge the work and effort of others
- Ask questions openly — there are no bad questions

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a workplace setting

---

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it
confidentially to:

**Email:** `dev@syntek-studio.com`

All reports are reviewed privately. Consequences for violations may include warnings, temporary
suspension, or permanent removal from the project, depending on severity.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub and Forgejo issues, pull requests,
discussions, commit messages, and any other communication carried out in connection with this
project.

---

## Acknowledgement

By contributing to this project, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct.
